Kenta Tanaka (D3) received the **Best Presentation Award** at the 70th JSAP Spring Meeting held in March 2023.

Presentation title: "Gate-controlled Kondo effect in graphene quantum dots"

The award recognizes his precise gate-voltage tuning of quantum dot coupling and the systematic demonstration of Kondo temperature variation. Congratulations, Kenta!
