"Ambipolar doping of MoTe₂ field-effect transistors and p–n diodes" has been published in *Applied Physics Express*.

Ambipolar doping of MoTe₂ thin-film transistors was achieved via ionic liquid gating, demonstrating p–n junction diode characteristics. This represents an important step toward functional integration of atomically thin TMD devices.
