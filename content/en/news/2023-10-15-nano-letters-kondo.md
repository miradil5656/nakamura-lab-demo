A paper by Kenta Tanaka (D3), Naoki Suzuki (D2) et al., "Kondo effect in graphene quantum dots with tunable coupling," has been published in *Nano Letters*.

The study demonstrates systematic gate-voltage control of the coupling strength and Kondo temperature in hBN-encapsulated graphene quantum dots. The results clarify the unique Kondo signatures of graphene QDs with spin-degenerate ground states, opening new avenues toward quantum information devices.
