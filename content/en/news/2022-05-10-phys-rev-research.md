A paper by Naoki Suzuki (D1), Daisuke Ito (M2) et al., "Ballistic transport in hBN-encapsulated graphene at low carrier density," has been published in *Physical Review Research*.

The study provides detailed analysis of ballistic transport near the Dirac point in ultra-low carrier density regimes in hBN-encapsulated graphene. Devices with mean free paths exceeding several micrometers are reported.
